Betfair Trading

This provides a portal to perform automated trading on the Betfair Exchange. It provides useful tools to help you make the most of your trading experience, including colourful analytics and a user-friendly interface. This utilises Betfair API to communicate with Betfair to perform trading operations.

Login







We do not store any confidential data. The purpose of this is to perform simulations of your trading strategy. Management of funds and other sensitive account information should be performed in Betfair to ensure security.
